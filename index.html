Los Angeles Police Department

Safety, Service, and Security

About

The Los Angeles Police Department mission statement is to protect life, property and rights of all people to maintain order and enforce the law by responding to both emergency and non emergency calls with efficiency, professionalism and integrity. The Los Angeles Police Department assist in life saving rescue measures and protecting the people of the State of California. To this end, we will go above and beyond in service, professionalism, integrity, humility and service to the citizens. “To protect and serve is more than just a saying, it's a calling and commitment.
